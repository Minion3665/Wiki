## 1a.3.a

$f(x) = x^2$  
$= \lim_{h \to 0}(\frac{f(x) - f(x+h)}{-h})$  
$= \lim_{h \to 0}(\frac{x^2 - (x + h)^2}{-h})$  
$= \lim_{h \to 0}(\frac{x^2 - x^2 -h^2 -2xh}{-h})$  
$= \lim_{h \to 0}(\frac{-h^2 -2xh}{-h})$  
$= \lim_{h \to 0}(h + 2x)$  
$= 2x$
